Crystal Collector!!




You will be given a random numbrt at the start of the game.
There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
You win the game by matching your total score to random number, you lose the game if you total score goes above the random number.
The value of each crystal is bidden from you until you click on it.
Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each crystal.
